# Concept Options

## Option A: Coffee Bar
Simple morning and daytime model.

## Option B: Cafe and Wine Bar
Day to evening model with higher average ticket.

## Option C: Aperitivo Bar
Small plates and drinks with limited kitchen complexity.

## Evaluation Criteria
- Startup cost
- Simplicity
- Margin
- Staffing
- Fit with location
- Risk
- Scalability
