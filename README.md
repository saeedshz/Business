# Business Operating System

This repository is the single source of truth for building, operating, improving, and scaling hospitality businesses.

Current focus:
- Corella existing business systemization
- Gamla Stan café-bar feasibility and launch planning
- Reusable Restaurant & Café Operating System (RCOS)

## Repository Structure

- `00_governance/` — rules, decision principles, versioning, AI workflow
- `01_project_constitution/` — permanent project constitution
- `02_glossary/` — business and operational terms
- `03_business_context/` — Corella and owner/operator context
- `04_strategy/` — strategic direction and expansion logic
- `05_finance/` — financial models, break-even, cost control
- `06_legal_sweden/` — Swedish permits, compliance and legal checklists
- `07_operations/` — operating model and daily management
- `08_sop_library/` — standard operating procedures
- `09_templates/` — reusable templates and checklists
- `10_projects/` — location-specific projects
- `11_ai_prompts/` — prompts for ChatGPT, Claude and review workflows
- `12_ai_outputs/` — model outputs and comparisons
- `13_archive/` — deprecated or historical material

## Operating Principle

Every document should be practical, modular, and usable by an owner-operator. If a recommendation cannot be executed, measured, delegated, or improved, it does not belong in the operating system.
